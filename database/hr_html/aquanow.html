Trading and Sales

Our traders are tasked with providing the most effective execution for our clients. Our trading team works in a fast-paced collaborative environment where each day presents new challenges and the opportunity to make a difference in the markets.

View Trading Roles

Technology

We are leveraging a broad range of technical expertise to build one of the most sophisticated digital asset trading systems in the world. View Technology Roles

Operations

Delivering the world-class service our customers expect requires the highest standards of delivery for our middle and back-office functions.
