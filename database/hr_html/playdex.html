Why Playdex

Playdex is an all-in-one gaming platform that offers gamers a seamless and enjoyable experience from start to finish. With our game discovery platform, you can easily find and try out new games, as well as get recommendations based on your preferences. Our NFT rental platform allows you to unlock rare and exclusive in-game items, without the need to purchase them outright. And with our leaderboards, you can compete against other gamers for prizes and bragging rights while improving your skills and having fun.

Our founding team comprises of serial entrepreneurs and early team members in other startups in the blockchain/fintech/gaming space (i.e., Coins.ph). At Playdex, you will work with some of the most talented engineers and thought leaders in the industry.


Engineering at Playdex

We have a team of world-class web engineers. We have complete, end-to-end ownership for every project we take on and expect the same from you. We review all major code changes through pull requests and are constantly pushing ourselves to do better.


Our work values and culture

We value high integrity, low ego, an affinity for continuous learning, and the ability to figure things out quickly. We're always open to being wrong, and we love to improve our work through reasoned discussion and constant feedback. We trust and respect each other tremendously, which makes doing all of this easy.
