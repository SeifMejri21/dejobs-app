About Callback

Callback is a one-stop platform for NFT communities. Our mission is to help create stronger and more engaged communities.

This page is unverified. We believe Callback is a good company for English speakers so we're sharing what we have, but we can't guarantee the accuracy of this data. It's gathered from public sources or our users, and not from the company itself. Pleasecontact us if you'd like to help fill out this page and get Callback verified!

Callback makes managing NFT communities easy by providing a powerful, interoperable, no-code NFT toolkit. Core tools include NFT creation and distribution, token-gated forms, raffles, and voting. Callback also provides a lightweight mobile wallet to help new users collect their first NFTs (gasless) and connect to Dapps with ease.

We've helped brands like Suntory, AMBUSH, WIRED, ANA NEO, Mitsubishi Estate, Shibuya City and Digital Garage create NFT solutions to enhance their communities.

Our team (currently 10) have graduated from Stanford and Harvard and have worked at companies like Palantir and Meta. We're backed by Sozo Ventures, gmjp, Digital Garage and angels including Keisuke Honda and Justin Waldron.
