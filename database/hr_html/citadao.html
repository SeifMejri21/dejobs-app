Who we are

We are the only web3 initiative that enables you to redeem the underlying asset and modify your risk tolerance based on the properties in your portfolio.
The CitaDAO team has significant backgrounds in global real estate, finance and technology.
We choose high-quality properties based on our knowledge of the market, careful assessment by our real estate background investors, and an exhaustive, ongoing screening procedure by a legal firm.

Our principle

Legality is a key component of the CitaDAO platform
CitaDAO received a legal opinion from a reputable law firm that states that the CitaDAO Knight Token, as well as the Real Estate Token, are not security tokens. This facilitates our exchange listings, ensuring deep liquidity for our Real Estate Tokens and Knight Tokens.
Global Real Estate is Undervalued
The global market offers numerous opportunities that are not limited by geographical or local market boundaries. This means that businesses can expand their reach and tap into new markets around the world. However, international commercial real estate can be challenging and volatile. CitaDAO will increase the value for Real Estate Globally by applying blockchain technology to increase the yield on the real estate and make it more capital efficient.
Safety & Privacy are our priorities
Users engage with the CitaDAO platform via a non-custodial wallet (Metamask), which stores cryptocurrency tokens under their complete control.
