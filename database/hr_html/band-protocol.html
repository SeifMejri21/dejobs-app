Blockchain Technology

We are a group of blockchain enthusiasts who strive to make decentralized applications as secure as possible for adoption. Our work begins with battle-tested and highly secure oracle technology that operates trustlessly to define the best security practices for the growing blockchain industry.

User-Driven Development

There's nothing worse than building a software that nobody uses. Therefore it's our #1 priority to listen to our users, develop relationships with them, and build solutions to solve their pain points.

Strong Technical Foundation

A bug contained in a single line of blockchain code can lead to millions of USD lost. That's why at Band, we favor technical knowledge and work with only the best engineers in the field.

Community Focused

One of the most important aspects of a crypto project is a community of token holders. We cherish all the supporters who stand by us, give us a push where we need, and grow together as a community.
