# Careers

Here at TradingLite, we develop state-of-the-art and bleeding-edge tech, pushing the boundaries of modern browsers and mass data processing.

Would you like to work with us and build cool stuff? Let us know!

We’re a remote team with flexible hours.

Send an email to careers@tradinglite.com , make sure to include a link to your portfolio.

(Frontend) Website developer [Svelte]

You have a talent for making beautiful and accessible designs, from concept to final product.

The frontend website designer position requires expertise in Svelte and experience with responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

  • Requirements:
    • Svelte knowledge required
    • Responsive design (mobile/tablet/desktop)
    • Design for modern browsers only
    • (optional) Sveltekit knowledge
    • (optional) Typescript knowledge
    • (optional) Experiencing with fixing Safari-specific bugs
(Frontend) App developer [Svelte] [Typescript]

You have a good background in creating web apps and love for creating complex extendable software.

  • Requirements:
    • Strong Typescript knowledge required
    • (optional) Svelte knowledge
    • (optional) Trading knowledge
    • (optional) WebGL knowledge (including shaders)
