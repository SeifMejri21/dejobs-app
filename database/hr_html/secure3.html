Secure3 Introduction​
Headquartered in Silicon Valley, Secure3 is an intelligent audit contest platform, aiming to reshape Web3 safety by providing effective, affordable, and secure smart contract auditing services for all Web3 projects.​
​
Secure3's audit contest organizes decentralized certified auditors through an audit competition. With 3-10x more auditors (compared to centralized auditing firms) working on the project independently and simultaneously, Secure3 has proven successful in providing cost-efficient and high-quality auditing services. In 6 months, Secure3 has done over 100 projects and has been recognized by industry leaders, including zkSync, Manta Network, IoTeX, ParaSpace, MirrorWorld, etc.​
​
Secure3 was incubated by Stanford StartX in 2022, and we received our seed round funding from top-tier US VC, crypto funds, and exchange ecosystem:​
​
Our team consists of the founding director of CertiK, security experts from SlowMist, Senior Tech Leads and Growth Hackers from Facebook, Amazon, and Airbnb, algorithm trading expert from Goldman Sachs, design leads from ByteDance and Airbnb, and top graduates from Yale, Stanford, Berkeley, CMU, etc.​
Secure3是来自美国硅谷，由斯坦福StartX孵化的的一个全新的竞赛审计平台，2022年获得Mirana，GGV，Alumni Ventures，Zonff Partners，HashKey等500万美元种子轮融资。半年内已经为超百个项目完成了安全审计，包括zkSync，Manta，ParaSpace，EthSign，MirrorWorld，P12等知名项目。​
我们的竞赛审计利用创新性的去中心化竞赛模式为客户提供最安全且价格实惠的审计服务，打破中心化审计不透明、昂贵、低效、质量差的魔咒，真正为客户解决安全审计的痛点。我们对自己的实力极其自信，敢于跟任何安全审计公司比拼审计质量。​
Secure3团队来自CertiK、Facebook、Airbnb、Amazon、高盛等，具有行业内独一无二的互联网、金融科技与区块链安全的跨领域交叉工作经验。团队成员毕业于耶鲁、斯坦福、伯克利等美国顶尖高校，人才密度高，自我成长空间充分，薪资待遇优渥，工作时间地点灵活。​
