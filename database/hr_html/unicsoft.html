What we love about Unicsoft

Employees

Unique caring & learning culture, rooted in shared values
Experience a flexible, humane & psychologically safe environment, encouraging our people to be themselves, speak up and share feedback. Our dedicated team is always at your service and ready to go the extra mile to make the work enjoyable for you.
Growth & career opportunities
We design roles to match the superpowers of our people, not just slot them into an orgchart. This ability is supported by our career planning & development system, mentoring and coaching.
Highest standards of management
Our best-in-class people management practices support autonomy, cultivate an ownership mentality and encourage personal growth. To ensure everything is progressing as planned we support managers with actionable feedback through a semi-annual management quality survey.
Empowerment
We create opportunities for our people to achieve more: personally, professionally, and financially. Your voice and ideas will be heard through monthly 1:1s, all-hands meetings, company-wide surveys, and our suggestion box, just a few of the platforms to gather team insights.

Independent contractors

Working on projects that make a difference
Only when you work on something meaningful, you feel truly fulfilled. At Unicsoft we choose projects very carefully to make sure they allow us to grow and make a positive impact. We work with large tech firms, global enterprises and startups with innovative ideas that will bring transformative change to the world.
Learning & development support
We provide unlimited access to leading online education platforms so you can build or polish your skills in trending technologies. In addition, we regularly conduct online/offline live training sessions and will keep you informed of what’s coming next.
Account management support
24/7 advocate of your interests before a client, ensuring that you receive all the information, support, and advice you need to do your best and enjoy your work 😉
Work where you want
Feel free to work from any corner of the world or out of our office in downtown Kyiv.
Competitive salary
Rest assured that you’ll be paid the going market rate and when the time comes, our dedicated account managers will help you negotiate a raise with the client and secure the best possible offer.
Activities to enjoy
Online English classes, summer/winter company days, dynamic internal Facebook group with daily announcements of upcoming sport and team-building events, masterclasses, and much more – we love hanging out together and having fun!
