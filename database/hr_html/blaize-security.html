ABOUT US

Founded in 2016, Blaize company covers all areas of blockchain and crypto development, including projects in DeFi, NFT-related products, blockchain ecosystems, protocols, and smart contracts. Blaize provides a wide range of services, including smart contract audits, custom blockchain development, tailor-made enterprise solutions, consulting, and much more.

We have completed over 70 successful projects in different areas of blockchain development and conducted more than 110 smart contract audits. Our clients are Aurora, 1inch, PeakDeFi, LeagueDAO, and Celo.
Our great team comprises over 65 professionals with the deepest expertise and world-class performance. We are sure that the key to success is a constant self-development and growth. And we do encourage it.
