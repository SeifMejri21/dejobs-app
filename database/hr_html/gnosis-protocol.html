Our History

In It For The Long Haul

We’ve been members of the wider blockchain ecosystem since the beginning, and we’re here to stay. Gnosis was founded in 2015 as part of ConsenSys, a globally leading blockchain production studio. Gnosis spun out to become an independent company in early 2017 and decentralized into a DAO in 2020.

The Gnosis ecosystem today counts more than 250+ team members across different organizations, including spun-out projects like Safe and CowSwap, and the Zodiac tool suite.

Community-Focused

Community at the Center

At the heart of Gnosis lies a deep respect for community. We pride ourselves on being long standing community builders in the Ethereum ecosystem. We run Full Node and LisNode, co-working spaces for decentralized technology initiatives in Berlin and Lisbon respectively. We host DappCon, a yearly conference for the Ethereum ecosystem. Being part of our team places you at the center of the web3 world.

Our Hiring Process

Review

Our HR team manually reviews all applications.

HR Interview

A 30 minute video call with one of our recruiters.

Hiring Manager or Technical Interview

A more in depth conversation about your background and experience, and motivation to join us.

Task or Technical Interview

If we need to further assess some specific skills we may require a small work challenge or a technical interview.

Final interview

A final conversation with a member of our core leadership team.
