About Atato 

Atato is a cryptocurrency wallet and custody provider. Our apps let individuals and businesses securely use DeFi, NFTs, and all decentralized applications. Our mission is to enable the next 100 million blockchain users. We partner with ConsenSys, Kaleido and are one of the regional blockchain leaders. Based in Singapore, founded in 2018, atato has delivered flagship digital asset projects in South-East Asia.

Our Vision
We aim to enable the next 100 million blockchain users. For a wider grasp, you can read more about it here. Also, you can listen to Frontier Fintech Podcast featuring our CEO, Guillaume Le Saint, talking about secure computing vision.

Our Benefits

We are remote-first since 2018, and offer attractive compensation including bonuses and stock-options. We offer flexibility and work life balance to our employees. We have no set work hours, and we encourage everyone to make use of our work anywhere policy. The company meets bi-yearly for workshops and outings, and we have quarterly time-off for the whole company to promote employee wellbeing. 

  • Work anywhere - we are 100% remote
  • No set work hours - you manage your own schedule!  
  • Unlimited paid holidays
  • Company Bi-yearly team building
  • Yearly bonus and Employee Stock Options (ESOPs)
  • Learning tools and opportunities
  • Health Insurance and Mental Health Program
  • Multicultural work environment

Our Values
We value transparency, openness and efficiency. We believe in giving our employees a good work environment and experience where they can be free to express themselves, experiment, and share their thoughts and ideas openly. We believe in shaping and developing the skills of each employee. As a company, we make sure everyone gets heard and that each individual can collaborate with each other. We encourage curiosity and instilling a growth mindset. We work fast, efficient and most of all, we have fun while accomplishing our goals as one team.
