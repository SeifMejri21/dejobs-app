Our Values

Principle

Freedom and responsibility. We operate on clear principles, not rigid rules, trusting our team know where the lines are. This balance fosters a dynamic, accountable, and effective work environment.

Efficiency

Mission-Centric Efficiency. We focus on the 20% of work that brings us 80% of the impact, and grow in the journey.

Transparency

Openness in Action. We uphold transparency in all work-related conversations, documents, and meetings, fostering trust and collaboration.

Natural selection

Organisms that are more adapted to their environment are more likely to survive and pass on the genes that aided their success.

Life at Chainbase Team

We‘re Chainbase. We’re the best choice to join the crypto data revolution. If you're driven by innovation and eager to shape the future, come work with us!

Hack Driven

Encouraging curiosity and innovation, and constantly pushing the boundaries in the Web3 & Crypto.

Working-Group

Teamwork at the Core. At Chainbase, we operate in small, collaborative teams, fostering synergy and effective problem-solving.

Context, not Control

We empower our team with the context needed to make informed decisions, fostering autonomy over micromanagement.

Never Give Up

Persistence is at the core of Chainbase's culture. We face challenges head-on and view every setback as an opportunity for growth.
