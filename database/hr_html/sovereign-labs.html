What we do

We make scaling simple. We build the Sovereign SDK, a simple framework for creating secure and interoperable zk-rollups atop a shared Data Availability layer. Developers will use the Sovereign SDK to deploy customizable and scalable rollups that communicate seamlessly.

Why we do it

We want to see blockchain applications scale to billions of users. Monolithic blockchains don’t scale. Rollups are a significant breakthrough in scaling, but existing designs are either very complex to build or unacceptably slow to bridge. Sovereign SDK rollups will be easy to build, and seamless to scale and bridge.
