About

ChainSecurity lives by the ethos of blockchain and fosters a culture of excellence through self-empowerment and accountability. We are a flat organization where employees are at the center of all decisions.

Our team consists mostly of PhDs and engineers from top Swiss universities and former Big 4 professionals.
