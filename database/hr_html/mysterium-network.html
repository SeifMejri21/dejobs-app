    What We Care About

    (Our Values)

    We play as a winning team

    We help our team members when they are in need. We encourage and celebrate mentorship. We ensure only constructive and honest feedback

    A hacker lives in each of us

    We experiment and push boundaries. In pursuit of progress we look beyond conventional ways.

    We are open, no bullshit

    We talk openly and honestly, safe to share our pains and joys with each other. We listen to different opinions, ideas and approaches. We openly acknowledge our mistakes 

    We radiate good vibes

    We like to be soft on people and hard on problems. The random and stupid jokes keep flowing throughout the day!
