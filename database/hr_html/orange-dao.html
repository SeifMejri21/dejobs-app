About Orange Fellowship

Our mission

Our mission is to bring more founders, builders and users to web3. We are doing this by investing in and supporting founders, and by building public goods to grow the ecosystem. Today, the work we do broadly falls into 3 categories

$50M Orange Fund

We invest in top-tier founders who are building in web3. We have already invested in 100+ companies

Supporting Portfolio

We support our portfolio companies with knowledge, hiring, connections, and mentorship.

Building Public Goods

We aim to build public goods to advance the web3 space. Example projects: Passport, LFG
