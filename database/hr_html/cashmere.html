Cashmere is a crypto wallet that enables companies to securely manage their crypto treasury. We are on a mission to provide access to the blockchain to any business that wants to be a part of the future. We are a well-funded, Silicon Valley startup backed by Y Combinator and Coinbase. We're a remote first company with the current team based on both the West and East Coast of the US.

Founding Engineer - Full Stack
Location - Remote

This is an incredible opportunity for the right person to have a massive impact on Cashmere business. We are growing insanely fast and looking for a strong full stack software engineer to join our world-class team, match the tsunami of demand we are already experiencing, and help us grow even faster.

You are a keen problem solver and team player. You take pride in the craftsmanship of your work, and appreciate when it is rewarded; our customers appreciate speed and attention to detail. The founding software engineering role at Cashmere encompasses architecture, design, implementation, and testing to ensure we build and release products in a way that upholds the quality of our brand.

A day in the life of a Founding Software Engineer at Cashmere includes:
‍
  • Being part of an inclusive, collaborative team that embraces healthy discussions and supports each other in their goals.
  • Architecting, designing, implementing, testing, and delivering robust and delightful products, continuing to keep the Cashmere experience best-in-class.
  • Mastering our development process, culture, and code base, then improving it. Continually leaving each of these in a better state than when you found.
  • Working closely with our design team to understand the correct trade-offs and fully execute the UI/UX vision for our applications.
The next great engineer at Cashmere will have:
‍
  • 5+ years of professional software development experience focused on frontend development
  • Deep understanding of web technologies such as JavaScript / TypeScript, MongoDB, Rust, NextJs, and Vercel. Our frontend is built with React and backend is in Rust. 
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Excited about the future of web3 and the opportunities it will bring

  • Medical, dental, and vision health insurance
  • Wellness/fitness & internet reimbursements
  • Top of the line 16-inch Macbook Pro
  • Opportunity to work with tech’s best talent; An incredible team with a great blend of hustle, productivity and a ton of fun!
  • Opportunities to attend the best crypto conferences in the world

Director of Business Development and Partnerships 
Location - Remote

You will be the first business hire for Cashmere. You'll be responsible for building and executing on a partnerships strategy that accelerates the next phase of Cashmere growth where you’ll gain exposure to the broader Solana Ecosystem and web3 in general. You'll work closely with our company leadership to identify partnerships that will help accelerate our product adoption, add to or enhance our product features, and expand Cashmere's user base into a new global market. The ideal candidate will have significant professional experience in a business development function with a focus on building institutional partnerships, in particular experience with business development around crypto custody solutions.We are a remote friendly team, looking for someone who aligns to US or Central European time zones.

A day in the life of a Director of Business Development and Partnerships at Cashmere includes:
‍
  • Define Cashmere's business development and partnerships strategy to drive the next phase of growth for Cashmere.
  • Work on sourcing, scoping and closing strategic partnerships to improve the user experience, add to Cashmere's value propositions as a product and onboard new users.
  • Engage and close potential partners across the web3, fintech, payments and tech spaces, and coordinate partnership execution across teams internally and externally.
  • Execute go-to-market strategies that launch new partnerships and product offerings with our partners
  • Track, analyze, and communicate the quantitative impact and business trends as they relate to partnerships
The next great business dev at Cashmere will have:
‍
  • 7+ years in partnerships, new business development or similar roles at a high-growth technology company
  • A track record of unlocking growth for a complex product/service via partnerships
  • Demonstrated track record of leading and closing successful partnerships with financial institutions
  • Experience in managing and executing multiple initiatives simultaneously
  • Stellar communication skills: establish clear goals and priorities and clearly communicate these to the broader team
  • Cross-functional experience working with internal and external stakeholders to execute partner projects and initiatives
  • Experience negotiating the commercial terms of strategic partnerships 
  • Strong analytical skills to build business cases and forecast potential impact of partnership opportunities
  • A strong passion and/or curiosity for web3
Bonus
  • Experience defining a partnerships strategy for consumer products
  • Experience growing a team
  • Experience working in international markets

  • Web3 experience
