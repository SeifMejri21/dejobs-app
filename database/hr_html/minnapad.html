Entertainment captivates and delights people all over the world. Has it made people truly happier, though? The world is still filled with conflict. According to a 75-Year study by Harvard University, happiness is produced by connecting with the people around you. What’s most important, though, isn’t the number of friends you have, but the quality of your relationship with those closest to you. We recognize that there are still challenges to overcome between people and entertainment as well as barriers between people that share in entertainment. Our purpose is to overcome these challenges and break thought these barriers to better connect fans of entertainment and make them happier.
